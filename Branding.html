1. The Coffe Nest

The logo for The Coffe Nest features a minimalist design in yellow and green, with a stylized coffee cup as its central element. The cup is simplified into a clean, rounded shape, and thin, curving lines rise from it to represent the hot steam, adding a sense of warmth and freshness. Above the cup, the words "The Coffe Nest" are displayed in a friendly, rounded font that forms an arc, embracing the cup symbol like a nest, emphasizing comfort and coziness.
The use of yellow and green conveys an inviting, vibrant, and natural energy, suggesting a space that’s both warm and lively—a perfect blend of relaxation and freshness, ideal for a coffeehouse setting.

2. Aconcagua

The Aconcagua Wine Brand logo effortlessly captures the spirit of Mendoza’s winemaking heritage, intertwined with the majesty of the Andes Mountains. The centerpiece of the logo is a striking artistic outline of the Aconcagua peak, presented in white to emphasize the purity and grandeur of this iconic summit.
The overall composition of the logo is a perfect balance of strength and sophistication, reflecting the brand’s commitment to quality and its deep connection to the land. The majestic mountain and the serene landscape beneath serve as a visual representation of the purity, power, and character found in every bottle of Aconcagua Wine.
